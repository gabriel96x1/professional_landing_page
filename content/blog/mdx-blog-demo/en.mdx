## What this post validates

The new flow checks three important surfaces:

- The home blog section shows real entries.
- The blog index lists metadata loaded from MDX.
- The article route renders the full post body.

## How to write an entry

Each language gets its own MDX file and its own slug. That keeps URLs natural for each audience and leaves a stable `id` to connect translations.

```ts
export const metadata = {
  id: "mdx-blog-demo",
  slug: "testing-the-mdx-blog",
};
```
